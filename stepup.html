STEPUP 5.0

Step Up 5.0 was an exhilarating annual 5k run organized by the RNSIT Sports Club. Taking place on June 11, 2023, it aimed to promote physical activity, community engagement, and environmental responsibility. Under the theme of "Planting for a Greener Tomorrow," participants joined forces to run while contributing to the environment. Funds raised during this event were dedicated to tree planting initiatives, fostering awareness about climate change mitigation, biodiversity preservation, and soil conservation.

STEPUP 4.0

Step Up 4.0 was a remarkable milestone for the RNSIT Sports Club, known for its impactful 5k run events. Held previously, the event carried the theme "You Run, We Plant." For each registration, a sapling was planted, underlining the synergy between fitness and environmental consciousness. Participants received donation certificates as tokens of their contributions to the cause. Step Up 4.0 highlighted the club's dedication to fostering positive change by merging wellness with environmental stewardship.

About Step Up Event

Step Up Event is an annual 5k run organised by RSC, under the direction of the RNSIT Physical Education Department. The event has different themes and purposes every year, but the goal is always the same - to promote physical activity, community involvement, and social responsibility. By organising a 5k run with the theme of planting trees, we can raise funds and awareness about how planting saplings is crucial for mitigating climate change and promoting soil conservation.

Themes and Impact

The themes of the previous events ranged from promoting environmental awareness, to supporting charitable organisations. Organising Step Up has become a tradition for RNSIT through RSC, inspiring individuals to take up running and exercise. Medals and certificates are bestowed upon all participants. Last year, Step Up 4.0 followed the theme "you run, we plant", where a sapling was planted for each registration, accompanied by a donation certificate. This year's focus remains "You run, We plant", with collected funds supporting the cause of planting trees and environmental conservation.

Goals and Benefits

Planting saplings not only supports environmental conservation but also contributes to biodiversity and soil conservation. Forests house various species of flora and fauna, and planting saplings restores habitats and promotes biodiversity. Trees help prevent soil erosion, landslides, and improve soil quality. Their roots hold the soil together and retain water, ensuring a sustainable water supply. By participating in Step Up, individuals actively contribute to these goals, making a positive impact on the environment and community.
